Draw

  • Click on Draw.
  • Press left click of mouse to start drawing the shape of 2D on the ground.
  • Press right click to complete.

Extrude

  • After drawing, click on Extrude.

Move

  • After clicking on extrude, click on Move.
  • Press mouse to select the 2D shape.
  • Drag mouse to move the 2D shape.
  • Leave mouse for 2D image to stay.

Edit Vertex

  • After clicking on extrude, click on Edit Vertex.
  • Press mouse to select any of the vertexes of the the 2D shape.
  • Drag mouse to move that 2D shape.
  • Leave mouse for 2D image to stay.
